Align, What, How and Why

We started off as a Mental Health Community with dedicated listeners, what followed was a gradual realisation of how inefficient the overall Mental Health Infrastructure is.

And from there we decided to do something about it. We came up with a three pronged approach.

1) Empowering Mental Health Professionals

Diagnosis and Management of Mental Health issues can only be done by a Mental Health Professional, We work with Mental Health professionals to create products that will help them do their job better. Empowering them means making sure professional help is available to the community. (new line) If you are a Mental Health professional, (contact here)click here).

2) Promoting Creativity

The modern day hustle and meaninglessness that comes with it. Nuerosis related to the modern lifestyle is quite an epidemic now. Our solution, Promote Creativity. There is growing evidence that engaging in creative works help you lower neurosis related to meaninglessness. We work with creative arts coaches and tutors to promote their works and techniques in the community. If you are engaged in any kind of creative endeavour and want to work with us (click here)

3) Awareness and Support

Finding help is good!, its a different ball game when you are not aware of your problem. Lack of awareness and support for various neurosis means people affected get no help, worse, they underplay their issues or arent even aware they have any. Our solution here is to making the community Aware of everything mental health by leveraging social media and other means. We are actively working on making a Mentally hygenic Community to help provide support people with who are overwhelmed with issues. Discord link etc.!
