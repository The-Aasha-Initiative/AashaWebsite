My Story

Being in the public eye, I know that it might feel a little frightening to contact a therapist. Please let me reassure you that all therapy is highly confidential. Asking for help is not the easiest thing to do - so I make the therapy as easy as possible for you.

My online sessions can give you access from virtually anywhere in the world - at almost any time.

My clients at Serene Lifestyles have felt completely transformed. They state that they feel it was the best thing they had ever done for themselves. Wonderful transformations happen here all the time.

I hope to be your go-to person. The person who can give you the confidence that you need and cutting edge techniques to rewire your mind for the better. You will have help and support on your chosen path.

Let me show you how to embrace your own self-worth and accept all that life brings to make you happy.

Having lost my father at a very young age and on the brink of the poverty line. I worked endlessly to better my circumstances. I studied, became successful and was happily married.

After trying to conceive for a few years, we embarked on the IVF journey. After several failed attempts and miscarriages, this invasive journey led us nowhere. It ultimately resulted in our ten-year marriage breaking down.

At that time, in that painful place, I decided to focus on my personal development and changed my career path, grasping this point as an opportunity to reboot my life and start again.

As a result of my own difficult journey, I know now that my purpose is to raise vibrations and give all my love, joy and positive energy. I'm passionate about helping people with their mental and emotional wellbeing

I can assist people who suffer from Stress, Anxiety and Depression. I can help individuals free themselves from addiction, self-sabotage, unexplained infertility, weight issues.

I can help liberate you from other problems, both physical and emotional, that are causing you to be unhappy, unsatisfied, distressed or panicky.

I'm thankful because I could not ask for a more rewarding career. I'm grateful to have had the opportunity to transform people from all walks of life so that they can feel their own sense of freedom, purpose, happiness and most of all peace of mind.

All of the treatments are designed for an individual's own, personal needs. This means it's impossible to state a 'One-price-fits-all' as that's not how real human beings work!
